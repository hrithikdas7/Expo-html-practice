About Us

Sambhav Exports is a story rooted in the soil.

Established with a vision to bring the best of Indian agriculture to the global market, Sambhav Exports has grown into a trusted name in the industry. We specialize in the Export Activity and Trading of Agriculture produces, ensuring that every shipment meets international standards of quality and freshness.

Our journey began in Nashik, the heart of India's agricultural belt. Over the years, we have built strong relationships with local farmers, allowing us to source premium produce directly from the fields.

Today, the fourth generation steps forward with a larger dream. With respect for our past and a vision for the future, we are expanding beyond borders to share India’s finest produce with the world. Along with onions, we proudly export grapes, green chillies, and capsicum, grown and sourced with the same care that defined our journey from the beginning.

At Sambhav Export, every shipment carries more than produce — it carries generations of trust, hard work, and passion from our fields to global markets.
