Who We Are

Sambhav Exports stands on a legacy of over 50 years in Indian agriculture. Starting as family-run onion cultivators in Nashik, we have evolved into a dynamic export house connecting Indian farmers to the world.

Our deep-rooted experience allows us to identify the finest quality produce directly at the source. We handle every step—from procurement and grading to packaging and logistics—ensuring our clients receive only the freshest, export-grade products.
